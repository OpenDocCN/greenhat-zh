A programmer I know thought he'd figured out how never to get a parking ticket. His three choices for personalized license plates were 1) 0O0O0O, 2) O0O0O0, and 3) I1I1I1. He figured that if a policeman did spot the car, the letter "O" and digit "0" look so much alike that it would be next to impossible to copy down the license plate correctly.

Unfortunately, his plan didn't work. The DMV clerk who issued the plates got confused and he wound up with a plate reading "OOOOOO."
