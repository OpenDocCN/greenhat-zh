A programmer once worked on a form letter generation program for a bank. The bank wanted to send out a special, personalized letter to its richest 1,000 customers. Unfortunately for the programmer, he didn't adequately debug his code. Even worse, the bank didn't check the first batch of form letters.

The result: The wealthiest 1,000 customers all got a letter that began, "Dear Rich Bastard."
